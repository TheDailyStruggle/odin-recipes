Lasagna

Description

The best damn lasagna you've ever had.

Italian grandmothers are currently investigating how this secret recipe was leaked to the world

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Mix up the sauce
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Layer it and throw it in the oven for an hour
