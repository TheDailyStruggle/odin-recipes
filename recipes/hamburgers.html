Hamburgers

Description

Dripping with juices, you will think you've found heaven on earth

Show your friends who the real grill master is with these beautiful burgers

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 1 head of lettuc
  • American cheese
  • Brioche buns

Steps

  1. Make the patties
  2. Slice the veggies
  3. Throw them on the grill
